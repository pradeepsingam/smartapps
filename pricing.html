Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All Core and Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to get started.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard.

What counts as a "screen"?

A screen is any display device (TV, monitor, tablet) running the Smart App player. Pricing is per active screen per month.

What hardware do I need?

Smart App works with our PIXI player, Smart App OS, or any Android, ChromeOS, Windows, or macOS device. We're hardware agnostic.

Do you offer discounts for large deployments?

Yes. Contact our sales team for volume pricing on 50+ screens. Enterprise plans are fully customised to your needs.

How does annual billing work?

Annual plans are billed once per year and save you up to 17% compared to monthly billing. You can add screens at any time pro-rated.
